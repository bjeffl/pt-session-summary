Supine Knee-to-Chest Stretch

Hamstrings / Lower Back
  1. Lie flat on your back on a firm surface
  2. Bend one knee and bring it toward your chest
  3. Clasp both hands around your shin or behind the knee
  4. Gently pull knee closer until you feel a stretch

Seated Hamstring Stretch

Hamstrings
  1. Sit on the edge of a table or firm surface
  2. Extend one leg out straight in front of you
  3. Keep your back tall and hinge forward at the hips
  4. Reach toward your toes until you feel the stretch behind the thigh
  5. Keep the other foot flat on the floor for stability
Hold 1 minEach sideDaily

Standing Calf Stretch (Lunge)

Calves / Gastrocnemius
  1. Stand in a staggered stance, one foot forward
  2. Keep the back leg straight with heel on the ground
  3. Bend your front knee and lean forward
  4. Raise the same-side arm overhead for added stretch
  5. Feel the stretch in the calf of the back leg
Hold 1 minEach sideDaily

Standing Gastrocnemius Stretch

Calves / Gastrocnemius

  2. Place the leg to be stretched behind you
  3. Keep the back leg straight, heel on the ground
  4. Lean forward into the wall, bending the front knee
  5. Feel the stretch in the calf of the back leg

Toe Spreads (Toe Splay)

Feet / Arch / Intrinsic Foot Muscles

  2. Spread all your toes apart as wide as possible

  4. Slowly release and repeat
